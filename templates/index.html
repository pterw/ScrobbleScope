Welcome to ScrobbleScope!

Discover your top albums from Last.fm with powerful filtering and sorting options:

  • Filter by Release Date: View albums from a specific year, decade, or all time
  • Sort by Play Count or Listening Time: See what you played most or listened to longest
  • Customize Thresholds: Focus on albums you really engaged with
  • Limit Results: View your top 10, 25, 50, or 100 albums
  • Export & Share: Download as CSV or save as an image

Default behavior: Shows albums you listened to in the selected year that were also released in that same year.
